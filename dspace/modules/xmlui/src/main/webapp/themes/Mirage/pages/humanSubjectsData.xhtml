Human subjects data

Data archived in Dryad are publicly available, and any human subjects data must be properly anonymized and prepared under applicable legal and ethical guidelines. When de-identifying your data, both direct and indirect identifiers need to be considered. Dryad does not allow any direct identifiers, but a dataset may contain up to 3 indirect identifiers.
