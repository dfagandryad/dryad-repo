Institutional sponsors

We gratefully acknowledge the support of our institutional sponsors. Contact the sponsor for more information about eligibility and/or to obtain a voucher code to use during submission. Please contact director@datadryad.org or see our payment plans for more information about becoming a sponsor.

Canadian Healthy Oceans Network is a university-government partnership dedicated to biodiversity science for sustainability of Canada's three oceans. Network investigators should contact Michelle Lloyd for more information about depositing data associated with their publications.

University of Rochester Libraries -- To obtain more information about how to get a Dryad submission voucher, please contact Kathleen Fear, data Librarian, kfear [at] library [dot] rochester [dot] edu.
