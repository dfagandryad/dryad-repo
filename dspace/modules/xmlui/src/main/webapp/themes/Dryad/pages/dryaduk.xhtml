About DryadUK

DryadUK was a JISC-funded project being run from the British Library and Oxford University, with assistance from NESCent, the Digital Curation Centre (DCC), Charles Beagrie Ltd., and the Research Information Network (RIN).

For more details about DryadUK, see the DryadUK page on the Dryad wiki.

           
        

DryadUK is assisting the further development of Dryad in the following ways:

Expanding Dryad

  • By establishing a UK mirror site
  • By expanding the service to include new publishers, journals and disciplines

Increasing Dryad sustainability

  • By developing a sustainable business plan
  • By establishing a framework for evaluating Dryad data usage

Adding value

  • Exploring ways to improve metadata standards for deposition, citation and annotation
  • Assigning DataCite DOIs to UK publisher datasets via the British Library.
